EcoCity: The Game

EcoCity is not just a game. It is the human experience. The Earth is burning. The polar bears are drowning. Vancouver will soon be underwater. To survive, humanity must adapt. Introducing “EcoCity”, a game where a user must carefully craft and manage a city to be both economically and ecologically viable. Utilizing cutting-edge graphics and revolutionary gameplay elements, a player can design a city limited only by their imagination. EcoCity is no mere city-building simulator, however. Users create their cities with the goal of becoming emission-free by the year 2040, and must manage the pollution output of their city, finding sustainable ways to house people and manage industry.
