EcoCity: The Game

EcoCity is not just a game. It is the human experience. The Earth is burning. The polar bears are drowning. Vancouver will soon be underwater. To survive, humanity must adapt.

EcoCity

The Four Pillars Will Save Us

The four pillars of EcoCity are the only thing standing between humanity and oblivion.

Conclusion

In essence, EcoCity teaches a player, be they adult or child, that the key to a brighter future is to eventually eliminate the pollutants a metropolis produces. All of humanity is currently balanced on the razors edge of ecological oblivion, and we must learn that to survive, we must adapt.
